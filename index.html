About Us

Are you passionate about technology, innovation, and the power of collaborative software development? If so, you've come to the right place. The FSW Club is a dynamic and inclusive student body dedicated to all things related to open source.

WHO WE ARE

Our club is a vibrant community of students who share a common love for open source software. We believe in the principles of transparency, collaboration, and the transformative potential of open source projects. Whether you're an experienced developer or just getting started with open source, you'll find a home here.
